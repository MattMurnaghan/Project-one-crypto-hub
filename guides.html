How does crypto work?

Financial Apps - Apps like PayPal or Venmo in the US, allow users to purchase cryptocurrency directly on the platform

Crypto Exchanges - Crypto exchanges offer a brilliant begginner option for those just starting out in the crypto space. With low costs, wide selections of currencies and the option to store directly on the exchange, they are a very popular choice.

Trading Apps - Trading apps like Robinhood and Webull allow you to purchase select cryptos on their platform with no direct comissions on sales. However, they do charge a spread markup on purchases, to what extent is unclear as they have yet to reveal this information.

Traditional Brokers - Some traditional brokers are now giving their users the ability to trade in cryptocurrencies the same way that they would manage standard stock portfolios.

Bitcoin ATMs - Bitcoin ATMs are a less common way to purchase purely bitcoin. They are not linked to any other currencies and are a simple way for people to get involved in the space.
