Crypto Exchanges

What is an "Exchange" ?

Before you start trading in crypto currency, there are a few things you should know about exchanges:

  • A crypto exchange is a digital market place.
  • You can buy and sell many different types of cryptocurrencies on an exchange.
  • There are multiple exchanges that you can use, but not all exchanges trade in the same cryptocurrencies.
  • Not all exchanges are protected by national or international finacncial insurance, so it is important to pick the right ones!

Binance

Binance is a very popular crypto exchange and is widely used in europe. It is one of the few protected crypto exchanges but it does have its drawbacks:

Pros

  • Over 600 cryptocurrencies for trade
  • One of the cheapest platforms to use
  • Fees are fully disclosed
  • Massive selection of transaction types
  • Beginner friendly guides available to users

Cons

  • U.S. customers can't use the Binance platform, and the Binance.US exchange is very limited
  • It can be overwhelming, even for experienced users
  • No built-in digital wallet (Offers access to third party wallet, Trust Wallet)
  • In regulatory trouble in several countries and under investigation in U.S.

Coinbase

Coinbase is a beginner friendly platform that is also protected and is popular in both the US and europe but can lack clarity on some of its processes:

Pros

  • Extremely user-friendly platform
  • Well-known and trusted by U.S. regulators
  • Multiple ways to purchase cryptocurrency
  • Provides users with their own digital wallet
  • Supports withdrawls to paypal accounts

Cons

  • Limited advanced options for expert traders
  • Fewer transaction types than other platforms

Krakyn

Krakyn is another highly popular crypto exchange, established by Jesse Powell in 2011. Powell has previous experience in finance, and that shows through krakyns well rounded, easy-to-use platform:

Pros

  • Easy to use platform witha playful feel
  • Supports fiat deposits and withdrawals via ACH (US customers) and SEPA (EU customers)
  • High-level security measures
  • Stores customers' funds in hot and cold wallets
  • Has a 24/7 live chat

Cons

  • It takes 1-5 business days for your fiat deposit to reach your Kraken account
  • No mobile application

Gemini

Gemini is a popular exchange, founded by The Winklevoss twins in 2015. It has a reputation as one of the safest cryptocurrency exchanges and was the first licenced Ether exchange back in 2016.

Pros

  • Regulated by the NYSDFS
  • Low fees, free deposits, and no charge for the first 30 monthly withdrawals
  • Two-factor authentication and cold storage
  • Mobile-friendly

Cons

  • Supports only one fiat currency (USD)
  • Has a smaller selection of tradable cryptocurrencies
  • You can only buy digital currencies via bank transfers
  • The interface is not suitable for beginners
